Portable House

In the Beautiful Era of Ams Prefab designs

Experience modern living on the move with Portable Houses by AMS Prefab — a perfect fusion of mobility, comfort, and sustainable construction. Designed for flexibility and built with precision, our portable houses are ideal for temporary or permanent housing solutions in remote locations, industrial sites, resorts, or as emergency shelters.

Built using advanced #steelframingtechnology and our patented #modularsystem, each unit is prefabricated to ensure strength, durability, and easy transport. These homes can be relocated without compromising structural integrity, making them the smart choice for dynamic needs.

Our portable houses are fully customizable — from 1BHK to 3BHK layouts — and come equipped with insulated walls, modern interiors, modular kitchens, bathrooms, and smart energy-efficient systems. Whether used for personal stay, staff quarters, or temporary housing, they offer all the comforts of a traditional home with the added benefit of mobility.

These structures are not only weather-resistant and thermally efficient but are also crafted using eco-conscious materials, aligning with #greenbuilding principles. Optional add-ons like solar panels, air conditioning, and custom furniture can be integrated based on project needs.

AMS Prefab Portable Houses provide an innovative and hassle-free living solution where speed, quality, and adaptability matter most. Quick to deploy and built to last, these units are reshaping the way we think about housing.
