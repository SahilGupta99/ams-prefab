"A" Frame Cottages

In the Beautiful Era of Ams Prefab designs

Discover the charm of A-Frame Cottages—a harmonious blend of rustic elegance and modern engineering. Perfectly suited for boutique resorts, luxury retreats, or serene getaways, these cottages are available in 1, 2, 3, and 4 BHK configurations, ideal for couples, families, and groups alike.

The iconic triangular silhouette of the A-Frame isn’t just aesthetically striking—it maximizes interior volume while maintaining a compact footprint. Natural light floods the interior through large, strategically placed windows, creating a warm, inviting atmosphere that connects you to the outdoors. The energy-efficient design ensures sustainability without compromising on comfort.

Built using advanced steel framing technology and AMS Prefab’s patented modular construction system, each cottage is engineered for strength, durability, and long-term performance—even in challenging environments. The modular system also enables fast, efficient installation with minimal on-site disruption.

Inside, you'll find thoughtfully designed open-plan living areas, cozy private bedrooms, and versatile layouts to suit various hospitality or personal living needs. Every space is optimized for functionality and relaxation, delivering the perfect escape from the everyday.

Sustainability is at the heart of the A-Frame Cottage. Eco-friendly materials and superior insulation reduce energy usage and environmental impact, making these cottages an ideal choice for conscious living or eco-tourism ventures.

Whether you're creating a luxurious mountain resort, a tranquil lakeside retreat, or a modern vacation rental, the A-Frame Cottage is a timeless and adaptable solution that elevates both design and experience.
