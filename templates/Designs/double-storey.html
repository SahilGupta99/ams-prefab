Double Storey Houses

In the Beautiful Era of Ams Prefab designs

Experience elevated living with our elegantly crafted Double-Storey Homes by AMS Prefab — a perfect synergy of space, style, and smart engineering. Designed for modern families and upscale projects, these prefab modular homes are available in flexible configurations, including 2, 3, 4, and even 5 BHK options to suit every lifestyle.

The vertical layout of our double-storey design maximizes living space without expanding the building footprint — ideal for urban environments or premium residential plots. Open-concept living rooms, spacious bedrooms on both levels, and dedicated balconies offer comfort and privacy for each member of the household.

Each home is built using high-grade steel framing technology and AMS Prefab’s proprietary modular system. This ensures enhanced structural integrity, seismic resistance, and faster construction timelines — all with reduced on-site disruption.

The interiors feature smart spatial planning, modern finishes, and ample natural lighting through large windows and open stairwells. Kitchens, living areas, and bathrooms are designed for functionality and elegance, while the upper floors offer peaceful bedrooms and optional study or work-from-home zones.

AMS Prefab is committed to eco-conscious construction. All materials are sustainably sourced, and our insulated panel systems help reduce energy consumption — lowering utility costs while maintaining year-round comfort.

Whether you're building a modern villa, duplex for extended families, or a luxury rental property, our Double-Storey Prefab Homes offer a fast, reliable, and stylish solution. Choose AMS Prefab to bring your vision of a smarter, greener home to life.
